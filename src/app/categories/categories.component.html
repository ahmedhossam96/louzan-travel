who we are

Founded in 2013 by four travel enthusiasts who shared a thirst for adventure, and a common mission to break the norm. Louzan travel came to life with a goal to urge people into stepping out of their comfort zone. While others were hopping on tour buses for touristic sightseeing, Louzan travelers would get on bikes and encounter locals; building human connections through cultural exchange. Louzan dedicates itself to covering all corners of the globe, bringing you a myriad of adventure trips to choose from.
